College Admission Information:
Admissions are open from June to August every year. Students must submit their 10th and 12th mark sheets, transfer certificate, passport-size photographs, and ID proof during admission.

Fee Payment Rules:
Students must pay semester fees before the 10th of each semester. A late fee of 500 rupees is charged after the deadline.

Attendance Policy:
Students must maintain a minimum of 75 percent attendance in each subject to be eligible for semester examinations.

Library Rules:
Students can borrow up to 3 books at a time for 14 days. A fine of 2 rupees per day is charged for overdue books.

Hostel Rules:
Hostel students must return to the hostel before 8:00 PM. Visitors are allowed only between 4:00 PM and 6:00 PM with permission.

Exam Guidelines:
Students must carry their ID card and hall ticket during examinations. Mobile phones are not allowed.

Bonafide Certificate:
Students can apply through admin office. Issued within 2 days.

Placement Cell:
Training sessions are provided. Final year students eligible.

Scholarship:
Merit and need-based scholarships available.

Transport:
College buses available. Register at semester start.
